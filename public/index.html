Tech Insight

Core Products: High & Medium Voltage Busbar Systems

Wetown is a leading manufacturer of comprehensive busbar systems, providing reliable power transmission solutions for power plants, data centers, and industrial facilities.

  • High-Voltage Isolated Phase Bus (IPB): Continuous enclosure design for generator-to-transformer connections (up to 30,000A+), featuring 99.9% shielding efficiency, elimination of phase-to-phase faults, and prevention of supporting steel structure heating.
  • Medium-Voltage Enclosed Bus (NSPB/SPB): Compact, metal-enclosed systems for power plant auxiliary circuits and industrial substations, providing high short-circuit strength and flexible configuration for complex layouts.
  • Advanced Busbar Accessories: Integration of Air Circulation Drying Cabinets (ACDC), PT/LA Cubicles, and Neutral Grounding Cabinets (NGC) to ensure long-term operational stability and equipment protection.
  • Specialized Cast Resin Busway (CRB): IP68-rated, fire-resistant (90min+), and maintenance-free systems designed for harsh environments like chemical plants, outdoor installations, and high-humidity areas.
  • Insulated Copper Tube Busbar (PJTM): High-reliability shield-insulation circular bus duct featuring superior skin effect performance, high mechanical strength, and compact design for space-constrained industrial substations.
  • Intelligent Low-Voltage Trunking (LV series): Modular, sandwich-structure design for high-rise buildings and data centers, offering easy tap-off units, low voltage drop, and superior heat dissipation.
  • Power Transformers: High-performance dry-type and oil-immersed transformers designed for power grids and industrial energy management.
  • Energy Storage Systems (ESS): Advanced battery energy storage solutions for grid-side, industrial, and commercial applications, supporting the transition to renewable energy.
  • Photovoltaic Materials: High-quality PV ribbons and solar components, positioning Wetown as a key player in the global solar energy supply chain.
