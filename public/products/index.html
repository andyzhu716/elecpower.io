High Voltage Busbar Systems

Isolated Phase Bus (IPB)

Application: Main generator loop (Generator to Transformer connection) in power plants (Thermal, Nuclear, Hydro, Pumped Storage).

Technical Specifications

Parameter Value
Rated Voltage 10kV – 36kV
Rated Current 3,000A – 30,000A+
Short-time Withstand Up to 300kA (3s)
Peak Withstand Up to 750kA
Enclosure Material Aluminum Alloy
Cooling Method Natural / Forced Air
  • Key Features:
    • Absolute Isolation: Each phase housed in a separate, grounded aluminum enclosure.
    • 99.9% Shielding: Continuous enclosure design eliminates external magnetic flux, preventing steel structure heating.
    • Safety: Eliminates phase-to-phase faults; high short-circuit withstand capability.
    • Structure: Large span support capability, minimizing civil engineering costs.

Auxiliary Equipment for IPB

To ensure the stable operation of the IPB system, Wetown provides a complete set of auxiliary cabinets:

PT & LA Cubicle (Potential Transformer & Lightning Arrester)

![PT & LA Cubicle](/images/wetown/PT & LA Cubicle.png “PT & LA Cubicle”)

  • Function: Provides voltage monitoring and overvoltage protection for the main generator loop.
  • Features:
    • Modular Design: Draw-out type for easy maintenance and testing.
    • Safety Interlock: Mechanical/electrical interlocking to prevent misoperation.
    • Protection: Equipped with high-capacity lightning arresters to suppress switching surges.

Neutral Grounding Cabinet (NGC)

  • Function: Connects the generator neutral point to ground via a high-resistance resistor to limit fault current.
  • Key Specs:
    • Rated Voltage: Up to 24kV
    • Rated Current: 5A – 20A (Typical)
    • Resistance: Stainless steel grid resistors with stable temperature coefficient.
    • Monitoring: Integrated intelligent monitor for ground fault detection.

Air Circulation Drying Unit (ACDU)

  • Function: Maintains positive pressure and dry air inside the IPB enclosure to prevent condensation and dust ingress.
  • Features:
    • Dual Mode: Automatic switching between micro-positive pressure and circulation drying.
    • Efficiency: Roots blower system ensures high airflow (up to 54m³/h) with low power consumption.
    • Intelligence: Real-time humidity and pressure monitoring with remote alarm capability (IEC 61850 ready).

Non-Segregated Phase Bus (NSPB)

Application: Medium voltage distribution in 600MW/1000MW power plants and industrial facilities.

Technical Specifications

Parameter Value
Rated Voltage 3.6kV – 40.5kV
Rated Current 630A – 6,300A
Short-time Withstand 40kA – 100kA (4s)
Peak Withstand 100kA – 250kA
Insulation Level 95kV (BIL) – 185kV (BIL)
  • Key Features:
    • Compact Design: All phases in a single enclosure without barriers; space-saving.
    • Cost-Effective: Economical solution for medium voltage distribution.
    • Durability: Robust aluminum/steel alloy enclosure for high mechanical strength.

Segregated Phase Bus (SPB)

Application: Power plant auxiliary power, start-up transformers, and industrial substations.

Technical Specifications

Parameter Value
Rated Voltage 3.6kV – 40.5kV
Rated Current 1,000A – 6,300A
Short-time Withstand 50kA – 100kA (4s)
Peak Withstand 130kA – 250kA
IP Rating IP54 / IP65
  • Key Features:
    • Phase Barriers: Metal barriers separate phases within a common enclosure, preventing fault propagation.
    • Protection: High IP rating (IP54/IP65) for indoor/outdoor use.
    • Maintenance: Easy access for inspection while maintaining phase isolation safety.

Specialized Busbar Systems

Technical Specifications

Parameter Value
Rated Voltage 0.4kV – 35kV
Rated Current 400A – 6,300A
Protection Rating IP68 (Submersible)
Fire Resistance F120 (120 min)
Impact Rating IK10
  • Key Features:

Insulated Copper Tube Busbar (PJTM)

Application: Space-constrained industrial substations and switchgear connections.

Technical Specifications

Parameter Value
Rated Voltage 0.4kV – 40.5kV
Rated Current Up to 12,000A
Insulation Material EPDM / Silicone Rubber
Partial Discharge < 10pC
Bending Radius ≥ 3D (Flexible installation)
  • Key Features:
    • Skin Effect Optimization: Hollow circular conductor maximizes current carrying capacity.
    • Solid Insulation: Shield-insulation structure allows for touch-safe operation (zero surface potential).
    • Mechanical Strength: High dynamic stability against short-circuit forces.
    • Compact Footprint: Allows reduced phase spacing, ideal for retrofit projects.
