Wetown power distribution equipment categories for industrial facilities and power generation applications.

## Product Categories

### Wetown Isolated Phase Bus (IPB)
Metal-enclosed bus per IEEE C37.23, with each phase fully isolated in its own enclosure. Suited for generator-to-transformer connections and other high-current, high-reliability applications.

### Wetown Segregated Phase Bus (SPB)
Common enclosure with physical barriers separating phases. Improves fault isolation and serviceability while sharing an outer housing.

### Wetown Non‑Segregated Phase Bus (NSPB)
Common enclosure without phase barriers. Cost-effective option for applications with lower fault isolation requirements.

### Wetown Busbar Trunking System (IEC 61439‑6)
Modular low-voltage busbar distribution (also known as “Busway”). Ideal for buildings, data centers, and industrial facilities requiring scalable, compact power distribution.
