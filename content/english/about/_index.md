**Wetown Electric Group Co., Ltd.** (Stock Code: 688226) is a leading provider of high-quality power distribution equipment, energy storage systems, and photovoltaic materials. We are committed to delivering premium products and solutions for new energy, industrial manufacturing, power grids, data communications, and commercial real estate sectors.

> **Mission:** "To provide safe, efficient, and intelligent power distribution solutions that drive the global energy transition."

## Milestones of Excellence

- **2004:** Wetown Electric Group founded in Yangzhong.
- **2015:** "WETOWN" recognized as a China Famous Trademark; Brand ranked among "Most Influential Brands in China's Electrical Industry."
- **2021:** Successfully listed on the **Shanghai Stock Exchange (STAR Market: 688226)**.
- **Strategic Partnerships:** Formed alliances with global giants like **ABB, GE, and Westinghouse**.
- **Iconic Projects:** Supplied core power systems for the **Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge**, **Bird's Nest (National Stadium)**, and **Beijing Daxing International Airport**.

## Business Scope

Our comprehensive product portfolio includes:
- **High Voltage Busbar Systems:** Including Isolated Phase Bus (IPB) for power generation (up to 30,000A+) and Segregated Phase Bus.
- **Medium Voltage Busbar:** Insulated Shielded Tube Busbar and Cast Resin Busbar for specialized applications.
- **Power Transformers:** High-performance transformers for efficient voltage management.
- **Low Voltage Busbar Trunking:** Compact, modular systems for efficient power distribution in buildings and industry.
- **Switchgear & Electrical Components:** Reliable medium and low voltage equipment.
- **Energy Storage & PV Materials:** Innovative solutions for the renewable energy sector.

## Quality Standards

All Wetown products are designed and manufactured to meet strict international standards, including **IEC** and **IEEE**. We ensure rigorous quality control and offer factory acceptance tests (FAT) to guarantee performance and safety. Our testing center is CNAS certified, ensuring the highest level of product verification.

## Global Reach

With a strong track record in international projects, Wetown delivers engineering excellence to power plants, industrial complexes, and commercial infrastructure across the globe.
